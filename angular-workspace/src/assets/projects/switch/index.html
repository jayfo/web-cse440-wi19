Enter What You Want in the Trip.
Switch Plans the Trip for You.

By using Switch, planning long-distance is made easy. Simply go through the planning steps as instructed by the application. Select drivers, route, vehicle, and stops to visit along the route

Go to settings to adjust Profile, Drowsy Alert perferences, Privacy, and Groups and Friend.

Tap on the trip to reveal action options. Tap on start trip and you're ready to go!

Features

Tools to improve your long-distance driving experience

Track Personal Driving Limit

Access your health data to calculate your maximum driving time before going into drowsy driving.

Incorporate Scenic Spots

Enter all the places you want to visit along your route when you use Switch to plan your trip. Switch will present to you the most efficient plan.

Customized Plan

Create a long-distance trip plan and switching schedule that best matches your vehicle's fuel efficiency and each drivers' driving limit.

Sensing and Alerts

Alert drivers during the trip to prevent drowsy driving.
