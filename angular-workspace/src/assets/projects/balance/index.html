What We Do Different

Easy Tracking

Inputting data into Balance is easy as cake, with its user-friendly interface designed for beginners and experts alike.

Aware

Balance is smart and aware of your progress. It motivates you when you're a little behind and congratulates you when you're doing great!

Flexible

Life happens. Balance adjusts to any sudden situations to keep you motivated and making progress.

Community Oriented

With Balance, you're immersed in communities of other goal setters with similar goals, to share progress and advice, and keep each other accountable.
